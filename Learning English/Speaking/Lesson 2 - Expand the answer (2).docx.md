### Example:

#### *Topic: Do you like cooking?*

Well, it really depends on my free time.  
**If I have a day off and feel energetic**, I usually **try a new recipe or cook something nice** because it helps me **relax, unwind, and treat myself**. I also enjoy **updating my cooking skills** and making homemade food.  
**But if I'm busy with work or feel exhausted**, I just **order food or make something quick** instead, because it’s **more convenient and saves time**, especially when I don't have the energy to cook or clean up.

#### *Topic: Do you like going to cafés?*

Honestly, it depends on my mood.  
**When I'm in a good mood**, I often **go to a café, study there, or hang out with friends** because I want to **enjoy the atmosphere, feel inspired, and boost my mood**. The environment helps me **focus and get new ideas** too.  
**But if I feel tired or need to save money**, I prefer to **stay home and make coffee myself** instead, since it’s **cheaper, more relaxing, and I can also avoid crowds**. Sometimes, it's just nicer to chill in my own space.

## **3\. Negative Answer** 

### Công thức

**1\. Thừa nhận thật lòng không thích / không làm (negative opinion)**  
**2\. Lý do (why not)**  
**3\. Giải pháp thay thế / lựa chọn khác (instead)** *(nếu có)*  
**4\. Tương lai dự định/giả định (optional)**

### Khung câu mẫu

####  *Mở đầu*

* To be honest, I don’t really \+ V…

* Honestly speaking, I’m not that into \+ V\_ing / N…

* To be fair, I rarely…

* Well, actually, I don't really enjoy…

* In fact, I hardly ever…

*Dịch nghĩa:* thật lòng mà nói/ thực ra/ phải nói thật…

####  *Lý do (Why not)*

* because I’m usually **up to my ears in work/study**  
  → tôi lúc nào cũng bận ngập đầu

* because it’s **time–consuming**  
  → tốn thời gian

* because it takes **too much effort / preparation**  
  → tốn công / tốn sức chuẩn bị

* because I'm **on a tight budget**  
  → tôi hạn chế chi tiêu

* because I don’t find it **enjoyable / necessary**  
  → tôi không thấy thú vị / cần thiết

**CẢM THẤY:**

**feel \+ tính từ: nói về cảm xúc của chính người nói: I feel happy: tôi cảm thấy vui**

**find \+ sb / sth \+ tính từ: nói về suy nghĩ / nhận xét của người nói về ai / cái gì**

**I find reading books boring and time-consuming**

**\= I think that \+ S \+ tobe \+ tính từ**

**I think that reading books is boring and time-consuming**

* because I’m a bit **lazy / unmotivated**  
  → tôi hơi lười / thiếu động lực

* because I prefer something **quicker / simpler**  
  → tôi thích thứ gì đó nhanh hơn / đơn giản hơn

####  *Giải pháp thay thế (Instead / rather than)*

* so instead, I usually...

* so I just…

* so I prefer to… instead

* so most of the time I end up \+ V\_ing…

*Dùng với hành động B*

Ví dụ hành động thay thế (xem phần it depends, câu thuận lợi)

#### *⭐ Dự định (Optional)*

* But in the future, I’ll try to…

* However, I might start doing it when I have more free time

* But I know it’s good for me, so I should give it a try (thử nó)

### Example:

#### *Topic: Do you exercise regularly?*

To be honest, I don’t exercise that regularly.  
I’m usually **up to my ears in schoolwork**, and honestly, I’m a bit **lazy** when it comes to (khi nói về việc) working out.  
So instead, I just **stretch at home or take short walks** because it's **quicker and easier**.  
But I know staying fit is important, so I’ll try to be more **consistent** in the future.

#### *Topic: Do you enjoy reading books?*

Actually, I don’t read long books very often.  
It’s quite **time–consuming**, and sometimes I find it **hard to concentrate** after a long day.  
So instead, I usually **read short articles or watch educational videos** because they’re **faster and more engaging**.  
But I **do** want to read more books when I have extra time.

##### Từ vựng hay để nâng band

| Cụm | Nghĩa |
| ----- | ----- |
| be up to my ears in work | bận ngập đầu |
| time–consuming | tốn thời gian |
| hard to concentrate | khó tập trung |
| lack motivation | thiếu động lực |
| prefer something quicker | thích làm gì nhanh hơn |
| engaging | thu hút, lôi cuốn |
