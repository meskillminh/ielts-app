# 📋 IELTS Speaking – Quick Review (All Lessons)

---

## ✅ LESSON 1 – Expand Your Answer (5WH1H Method)

### 3 Ways to Answer
| Method | When to use |
|---|---|
| **Answer + 5WH1H** | Most questions |
| **It depends** | Questions that vary by situation |
| **Negative answer** | When you genuinely don't like something |

### Step 1: Answer directly (paraphrase the question!)
- Yes/No question → Yes/No + paraphrase  
  *"Do you like music?" → "Yes, I'm really into listening to music."*
- WH question → Answer directly  
  *"When do you watch TV?" → "I often watch TV at night."*

> ⚠️ Never repeat the exact words from the question. Use synonyms!

### Step 2: Expand with 5WH1H

| WH | Useful phrases |
|---|---|
| **WHO** | along with my close friends / my family members |
| **WHERE** | near my neighborhood / within walking distance from my house |
| **WHEN / HOW OFTEN** | every now and then / whenever I have free time / twice a week |
| **WHAT** | especially… / something like… / all kinds of… |
| **HOW** | spend a lot of time on… / use my phone to… |
| **WHY** | *(see below)* |

### WHY – Useful Phrases
| Purpose | Phrase |
|---|---|
| Relax | unwind / clear my mind / recharge my batteries / relieve stress |
| Self-improvement | broaden my knowledge / sharpen my skills / step out of my comfort zone |
| Health & mood | keep my body in shape / lift my mood / give me a sense of freshness |
| General | a good way to kill time / my all-time favorite |

### Band 6.5 Grammar Boosters
- Relative clause: *"which is one of the most popular sports in my country"*
- Not only… but also…
- Present perfect: *"I've been doing it for a few years now"*
- Collocations: *stay in shape, improve my concentration*

## ✅ LESSON 2 – Expand the Answer (2 More Methods)

### Method 2: "IT DEPENDS"
**Template:**
1. *"Well, it really depends on my [mood / schedule / budget / energy level / weather]."*
2. *"If I [condition A], I usually [action A] because [reason]."*
3. *"But if I [condition B], I [action B] instead, because [reason]."*

**Condition A (positive):** have free time / feel energetic / be in a good mood / have enough money / it's the weekend  
**Action A:** go out / hang out with friends / try a new recipe / exercise / read a book  
**Reason A:** relax / unwind / stay fit / boost my mood / bond with friends / treat myself  

**Condition B (negative):** feel tired / be up to my ears in work / have a tight schedule / feel under the weather / am broke  
**Action B:** stay home / order food / watch Netflix / scroll social media / take a nap  
**Reason B:** save money / save time / rest my body / avoid traffic / keep things simple  

**Example:**
> *"It really depends on my free time. If I have a day off and feel energetic, I usually try a new recipe because it helps me relax and treat myself. But if I'm busy with work, I just order food instead, because it's more convenient."*

---

### Method 3: NEGATIVE ANSWER
**Template:**
1. **Admit** you don't like it → *"To be honest, I don't really…"*
2. **Reason why not**
3. **Alternative** (what you do instead)
4. **Future plan** (optional)

**Opening phrases:**
- To be honest, I don't really + V…
- Honestly speaking, I'm not that into + V-ing…
- In fact, I hardly ever…

**Reasons (why not):**
- I'm usually up to my ears in work/study
- It's time-consuming
- It takes too much effort
- I'm on a tight budget
- I don't find it enjoyable / I find it + adj

**Alternatives:** so instead, I usually… / so I just… / so I prefer to… instead

**Future:** But I know it's good for me, so I'll try to… / But I might start doing it when I have more free time.

**Example:**
> *"To be honest, I don't exercise that regularly. I'm usually up to my ears in schoolwork, and honestly, I'm a bit lazy when it comes to working out. So instead, I just stretch at home because it's quicker and easier. But I know staying fit is important, so I'll try to be more consistent in the future."*

## ✅ LESSON 3 – Basic Information (Part 1 Topics)

### I. HOMETOWN

**Where is your hometown?**
> *"I was born and raised in [city], which is a rather [lively/peaceful/vibrant/picturesque] city located in the [north/south/central part] of Vietnam. It is best known for [key feature]. At the moment, I'm still living there / I've moved to [city] for work/study."*

**Key features:** beautiful coastline / traditional food / historical sites / exciting nightlife  
**Lifestyle adjectives:** laid-back / slow-paced / energetic / hustle and bustle

**What do you like most? → P.E.E Method**
- **Point:** what you like most
- **Explanation:** why it's special
- **Feeling:** how it makes you feel

> *"What I treasure most about my hometown is [X]. To be more specific, [detail]. For example, [example]. This is probably why I always feel a strong sense of belonging whenever I'm there."*

**What do you dislike?**
> *"To be honest, I love my hometown, but I don't really like the [heavy traffic / hot weather / noise / lack of facilities]. It is a big problem because [reason]. So I find it quite [annoying/tiring/stressful/inconvenient] sometimes."*

---

### II. THE AREA YOU LIVE IN

**Which city are you living in now?**
> *"Right now, I'm living in [city], in [District], which is a pretty [modern/bustling/peaceful] area. It's well-known for its [street food / friendly people / lively atmosphere]. I chose to live here because [job opportunities / convenient for studies / family moved here]."*

**Do you like living there?**
> *"Absolutely, I really enjoy living here because of its convenience. Everything I need is within walking distance of my apartment. For example, I can easily [commute to work / hang out with friends / find food late at night]."*

**What do you dislike?**
> *"Well, one thing I'm not really a fan of is the [traffic / noise / air pollution / high living costs]. It can be quite [inconvenient/stressful/time-consuming], especially during rush hours."*

---

### III. WORK OR STUDY

**Are you a student or do you work?**
- Student: *"I'm currently a full-time student. I'm majoring in [subject] at [university]. I'm in my [first/last] year now."*
- Worker: *"At the moment, I'm working as a [job]. My company is located in [city], and I've been working there for about [X] years."*

**Why did you choose that major/job?**
- Student: *"I chose this major because it's very practical and will help me find a good job after I graduate."*
- Worker: *"I decided to do this job because it fits my skills and personality. The working environment is also very professional."*
- Other reasons: passionate about it / stable career / inspired by family / good salary

**What do you dislike?**
- Student: heavy workload / early morning classes / difficult subjects / boring lectures
- Worker: long working hours / low salary / long commute / high pressure

**What are your future plans?**
- Student: *"My main goal is to graduate with a good degree. After that, I plan to [find a stable job / study for a Master's / work for a foreign company / start my own business]."*
- Worker: *"I plan to [get a promotion / learn a new skill / save enough money / change my career path] within the next 5 years."*
